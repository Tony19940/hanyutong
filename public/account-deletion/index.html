Bunson Teacher Account & Data Deletion

If you want us to delete your Bunson Teacher account or associated learning data, email lz3959823@gmail.com.

What to Include

  • Your username, if you registered one
  • Your display name or any identifying details you used in the app
  • A short message confirming that you want your account and learning data removed

What We Delete

  • Registered account credentials
  • Learning progress, bookmarks, check-ins, and related profile data
  • Associated uploaded avatar assets where applicable

Some limited records may be retained when required for security, fraud prevention, or legal compliance.

Guest Users

If you only used the app as a guest and never registered an account, uninstalling the app and clearing local app data will remove the session stored on your device. If you are unsure whether you registered, email us and we will help verify it.
