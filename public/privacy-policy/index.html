Bunson Teacher Privacy Policy

Last updated: 2026-04-15

Bunson Teacher is a Chinese-learning application for Khmer-speaking learners. This page explains what data we collect, how we use it, and how to contact us about privacy or deletion requests.

Information We Collect

  • Guest session or registered account information such as username, password, display name, and avatar choice
  • Learning progress such as learned words, bookmarks, quiz activity, check-ins, and streaks
  • Voice-practice data such as microphone audio, dialogue session state, and generated audio replies
  • Technical and advertising data such as app session tokens, basic diagnostics, and advertising identifiers used by Google AdMob

How We Use Data

  • To provide guest access, registered accounts, learning progress, and voice features
  • To show ads and reward temporary ad-free time
  • To improve app reliability, analyze usage, and prevent abuse

Advertising

The Android app uses Google AdMob for banner ads and rewarded ads. Rewarded ads may unlock temporary ad-free time. Depending on your region, the app may show a consent message before requesting ads.

Third-Party Services

We may use cloud hosting, database providers, Google AdMob, Google UMP, speech providers, and AI providers to operate the app. These services process data only as needed to support the features you use.

Your Choices

  • You can continue as a guest or register an account later
  • You can decline microphone permission and keep using non-voice features
  • You can request account or data deletion by email
